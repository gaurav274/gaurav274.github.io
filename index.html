Research Interests

I'm interested broadly in the intersection of systems and machine learning. The high-level objective is to democratize deep learning. Specifically, my research focuses on developing video database management systems (think MySQL for videos). It supports a declarative language similar to SQL and a wide range of commonly used computer vision models. I design approximate algorithms with statistical guarantees to improve the throughput of visual queries by introducing sampling, filtering, and caching techniques without compromising accuracy.

Representative Projects

  • EVA: Database system for exploratory video analytics.
  • Mamba: Approximate query processing in video analytics with statitical guarantees.
